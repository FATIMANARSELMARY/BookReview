The Alchemist

Summary

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a philosophical novel that follows Santiago, a young Andalusian shepherd, on his quest to find a hidden treasure in the Egyptian pyramids. Guided by dreams, omens, and a mysterious alchemist, Santiago embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Along the way, he learns about the Soul of the World, the importance of following one's Personal Legend, and the interconnectedness of all things. The novel emphasizes the themes of destiny, faith, and the pursuit of one's true purpose, ultimately revealing that the real treasure lies within.
